# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed.
